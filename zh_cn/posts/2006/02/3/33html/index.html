VIM TIP23

by Jonny Hou on #tips, #vim, 2006-02-03 05:00

Check out your .vimrc. If 'set notitle' is an entry, comment it out with a quotation mark ("). Now your xterm should inherit the title from Vim. 检查你的配置文件.vimrc.如果设置了set notitle, 请用" 注释掉。现在在图形界面x终端下可以显示Vim的标题了
e.g. 'Vim - ~/.vimrc'. This can be quite nice when programming and editing lots of files at the same time. by [jonasbn@wanadoo.dk] 例如：Vim－~/.vimrc . 这样在编程和编辑多个文件时会非常方便。
